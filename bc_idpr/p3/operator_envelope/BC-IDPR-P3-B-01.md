## 1. Purpose

\[
\ker D\mathcal P=\operatorname{im}D\mathcal G,

P3-B now asks a different question:

## 9. Independent invariant audit

Let

\[
X_c=X-\tfrac12\operatorname{Tr}(X)I,
\qquad
Y_c=Y-\tfrac12\operatorname{Tr}(Y)I.
\]

Define the centered normalized correlation

\[
c(\theta)
=
\frac{\operatorname{Tr}(X_cY_c)}
{\|X_c\|_{\mathrm{HS}}\|Y_c\|_{\mathrm{HS}}}.
\]

This scalar is invariant under:

- simultaneous orthogonal conjugation;
- common identity shifts;
- common nonzero scalar normalization.

For the declared family,

\[
c(\theta)=\frac{1-[3]_{\theta}}{1+[3]_{\theta}}.
\]

At the anchor,

\[
c(\theta_0)=0,
\qquad
c'(\theta_0)=2\neq0.
\]

This independently proves that the family changes modulo the declared nuisance group. It does not rely on the projection-rank calculation.
