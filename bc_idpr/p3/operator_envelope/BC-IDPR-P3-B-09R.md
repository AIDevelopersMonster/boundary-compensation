## 1. Provenance clarification

The duplicate M7 session followed a Windows Update restart and the opening of a new browser that resumed the same task. This workflow event is recorded separately and has no evidential effect.

The first M9 preregistration was invalidated before model fitting because the class with every doubled spin restricted to `{5,6}` contained no exactly-two-channel carrier. M9R is a new preregistration, not a post-hoc modification of M9.

## 2. Independent train and test classes

Training uses the nine representation families generated by valid ordered carriers with doubled spins in `{1,2,3,4}`.

Testing uses every valid family with doubled spins in `{1,...,6}` that:

1. contains at least one new label `5` or `6`;
2. is absent from the training family set;
3. has exactly two admissible channels in both declared pairings.

This gives 75 ordered training carriers in nine families and 208 ordered test carriers in fifteen new families.

## 3. Permutation-equivariant descriptor

For each unordered external-spin family, features are aggregated over all valid ordered realizations. The scalar baseline contains orbit means and standard deviations of normalized external and channel Casimir quantities.

The augmented descriptor additionally contains orbit means and standard deviations of the four entries of `F(theta0)`, its trace, determinant and entry second moment. Derivatives of `F` are excluded from predictors.

Targets are family means of

\[
\log|\omega|,\qquad \omega'/\omega,\qquad \omega''/\omega.
\]

Ridge regularization is frozen at `alpha=1`; standardization uses training families only.

## 4. Result

Scalar-family test NRMSE:

\[
(0.908351,\;3.932189,\;8.046650).
\]

Permutation-equivariant test NRMSE:

\[
(0.491553,\;1.584959,\;5.179159).
\]

Relative improvements are

\[
(45.89\%,\;59.69\%,\;35.64\%).
\]

Thus orbit statistics improve all three independent new-label targets. However, the frozen absolute requirement was NRMSE at most `0.40` for every target. None passes, and higher-jet errors remain large.

Numerical jet instability under halving the extraction step is at most `2.06e-7`, well below the registered `1e-4` threshold.
