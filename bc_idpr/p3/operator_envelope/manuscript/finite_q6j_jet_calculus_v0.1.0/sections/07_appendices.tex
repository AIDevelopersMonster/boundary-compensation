\section{First three generator derivatives}
Direct differentiation of $K=F'F^T$ gives
\begin{align}
K&=F'F^T,\\
K'&=F''F^T+F'F'^T,\\
K''&=F'''F^T+2F''F'^T+F'F''^T.
\end{align}
These formulas agree with Theorem \ref{thm:generatorrecurrence} after converting between normalized Taylor coefficients and ordinary derivatives.
