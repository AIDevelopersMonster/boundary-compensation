3. Approximate transfer margin

The transfer survives only when the transported macro-separation exceeds the target decoder modulus evaluated at the transported descriptor diameter.

A negative margin may mean numerical distortion, but it may also mean structural macro collapse. The latter cannot be repaired by reducing numerical error.
