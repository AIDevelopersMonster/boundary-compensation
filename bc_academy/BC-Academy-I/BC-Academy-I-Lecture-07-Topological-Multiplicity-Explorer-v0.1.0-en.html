Conditions behind the displayed formula

A1. The integer n is fixed inside the parameter chamber.
A2. Minimal monopole model on S2; no accidental zero modes.
A3. epsilon(alpha) is positive and C1.
A4. B0 = epsilon Cn with Cn strictly positive and C1.
A5. The chosen observable is determinant-based and logarithmically additive.
A6. Heavy-sector couplings are absent or absorbed into a regular Schur--Feshbach factor G(x).
A7. beta_epsilon and G'/G are bounded in the declared regime.
A8. No zero crossing, rank change, branch loss, or topological-sector change occurs.
